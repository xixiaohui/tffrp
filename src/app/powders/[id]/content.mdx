# Glass Fiber Powder Technical Data Sheet (TDS)

Glass Fiber Powder is a fine, milled form of E-glass or C-glass fiber, widely used as a functional filler and reinforcement additive in thermoplastics, thermosets, coatings, adhesives, and composite compounds. It improves mechanical strength, dimensional stability, thermal resistance, and abrasion properties without compromising surface finish.

## Processing Guidelines

- Ensure uniform dispersion using **high-speed mixers, twin-screw extruders, or ball mills**  
- Match sizing treatment with **matrix resin type** (e.g. silane for epoxy, polyester, or nylon)  
- Control moisture content below **0.2%** to avoid voids and bubbles  
- Powder form allows **high loading ratios** (up to 30% wt) depending on application  
- Wear dust control gear during bulk handling to comply with **safety standards**

## Quality Control & Standards

- Particle size distribution via **laser diffraction or sieve analysis**  
- LOI and moisture content per **ASTM D3171 / ISO 1887**  
- Appearance, color tone, bulk flowability inspection  
- Fiber glass integrity (no over-ground fiber balls or fused clusters)  
- Resin compatibility testing based on **customer formulation**
