# Glass Fiber Yarns Technical Data Sheet (TDS)

Glass Fiber Yarns are continuous filament strands composed of E-glass or other specialty glass materials. These yarns are primarily used as reinforcement media in weaving, braiding, knitting, electrical insulation, and composite production. With high tensile strength, electrical resistance, and thermal stability, glass fiber yarns are critical for aerospace, electronics, filtration, and textile applications.

## Handling & Storage Guidelines

- Store in **dry, shaded, and well-ventilated environments**  
- Avoid moisture absorption; reseal after opening  
- Use gloves during handling to avoid glass fiber skin irritation  
- Avoid excessive tension or mechanical stress during rewinding or transfer  
- Use within **12 months** for best weaving and adhesion performance
