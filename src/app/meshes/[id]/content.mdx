# Glass Fiber Meshes Technical Data Sheet (TDS)

Glass fiber meshes are engineered grid-like reinforcements made from E-glass yarns, widely used in construction, waterproofing, EIFS (External Insulation Finishing Systems), and industrial reinforcement layers. These meshes provide dimensional stability, tensile strength, crack resistance, and excellent adhesion with cementitious or polymer-based systems.

## Processing Instructions

- Mesh is embedded into a **fresh wet layer of mortar or adhesive** using a flat trowel  
- Ensure the mesh is placed with **minimum 10 cm overlap** between strips  
- Cut cleanly to avoid fraying; edge sealing is available on request  
- Ensure **coating is not burned or degraded during handling**

## Quality Assurance

All batches undergo tests per relevant international standards:

- **Tensile strength** before and after alkaline soak (EN 13496)  
- **Grid spacing and coating adhesion** validation  
- **Weight and thickness** uniformity check  
- **Shrinkage and deformation** under heat and humidity  
- **Visual inspection** for yarn breakage, coating defects, edge fuzz
