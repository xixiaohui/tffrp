# Glass Fiber Tapes Technical Data Sheet (TDS)

Glass Fiber Tapes are woven narrow fabrics made from continuous filament E-glass yarns. These tapes provide excellent mechanical strength, thermal insulation, electrical resistance, and chemical durability. They are widely used for pipe wrapping, electrical insulation, structural reinforcement, thermal protection, and marine/automotive applications.

## Handling & Storage Guidelines

- Avoid creasing or over-bending the tape during application  
- Store in a **dry and ventilated area**, avoid moisture or direct sunlight  
- Coated versions should be kept away from sharp tools or solvents that may degrade finish  
- Use **PPE (gloves, mask)** when handling dry tapes to prevent glass fiber irritation  
- Recommended shelf life: **12–24 months**, depending on coating type
